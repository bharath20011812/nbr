Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who is the founder of NBR Group?

NBR Group’s founder and Managing Director is Mr. Nagabhushana Reddy. With over two decades of experience, he has spearheaded the company’s journey in the real estate sector, focusing on delivering high-quality housing solutions across mid-segment and luxury markets. You can find more about his professional profile on LinkedIn.

2. What is the full form of NBR?

NBR stands for "Nagabhushana Reddy" Group, a real estate company with over 25 years of experience in delivering homes. The company has ventured into mid-segment housing and luxury hi-rise communities, ensuring diverse housing solutions across the real estate market.

3. Where are NBR Group’s projects located?

NBR Group’s real estate projects are located primarily in Bengaluru. Key project locations include Sarjapur, Mullur, and Nandi Hills, offering a variety of residential options for potential homeowners and investors alike.

4. When was NBR Group established?

NBR Group was established over 25 years ago, in 1998. Since then, it has grown steadily, offering real estate solutions across multiple segments in Bengaluru, earning a reputation for reliability and innovation.

5. Why choose NBR Group for real estate investment?

NBR Group is a trusted name in the real estate industry, known for modern, functional designs and high-quality developments. With a focus on new-age development, it ensures innovative solutions that meet the demands of today's homebuyers.

6. Will NBR Group offer discounts on villa plots?

NBR Group focuses on providing world-class construction and value to its customers rather than offering discounts. Their commitment is to deliver superior quality homes and villa plots with long-lasting value.

7. Who are the key stakeholders in NBR Group?

The key stakeholders of NBR Group include a dedicated team of real estate professionals, experienced architects, and engineers. For more information about the team, you can visit the official NBR Group website.

8. How can I book a property with NBR Group?

To book a property with NBR Group, you can connect with their team by sending an inquiry to info@nbrgroup.in. Their team will guide you through the process and provide necessary details.

9. What are the ongoing projects by NBR Group?

NBR Group is currently working on a new hi-rise residential project located at Sarjapur Road, Bengaluru. This project will offer modern living spaces with premium amenities.

10. What types of properties does NBR Group offer?

NBR Group offers a wide range of properties, including residential plots, mid-range homes, and luxury hi-rise apartments. They cater to diverse needs, ensuring that both mid-segment and luxury buyers find suitable housing solutions.

11.Where can I find NBR Group reviews and customer testimonials?

Customer reviews and testimonials for NBR Group can be found on their official website, as well as on various real estate review platforms. These testimonials provide insights into customer experiences and satisfaction.
