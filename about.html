Who We Are

About Our Real Estate Firm

Founded in 1998, NBR Group is a dedicated and innovative real estate developer located in Bangalore. We specialize in creating and developing land plots in Bangalore for residential and business purposes. NBR Group’ aim has always been to provide its customers with better and smarter spaces with a variety of modern amenities that upgrade the living experience of its residents.

Our idea of delivering gated communities at the most affordable prices stems from our passion. We’ve designed and developed communities in a way that owning a dream home is no longer a distant dream for people no matter their budget.

Our excellent quality, affordable housing and innovative ideas have made NBR Group excel in the industry. In 17 years of being in the industry, we are now known as the real estate developer of choice in Bangalore.

Leadership

Managing Director and Founder of NBR Group, Mr. Nagabhushan Reddy is a visionary who is guided by his love for nature. A true Bangalorean at heart, he wishes to see a greener Bengaluru and is willing to do his bit to ensure the same. At NBR Group, he has inculcated a culture of appreciation for nature and eco-friendly operations.

In his role as Managing Director, he has succeeded in taking NBR Group to its pinnacle. Mr. Nagabhushan Reddy brings a lot of zeal to the table, through which he is able to plan and crave the way forward. He believes that not only should everyone have a home of their own, he believes that they should have a home without compromising on budget or quality.

Chairman, Mr. Ashwath Narayana Reddy is one of the founding members of NBR Group. Since its inception, he has been committed to developing integrated communities that have all the amenities within the project. His beliefs and decades of experience in the industry have helped NBR Group to turn his dream into reality. Mr. Ashwath Reddy’s desire has always been to provide NBR Group’ customers with a ‘Live, Work and Play’ environment. He believes in building strong relationships with customers, which he says is the foundational fabric of every organization. His passion, vigor and determination are enviable and unmatched!
